Mission:

"to cultivate human powered outdoor recreation in Eastern Oregon through development, outreach, and stewardship."

Some of the work we are doing to bring recreation opportunities to Eastern Oregon:

Collaborating with local and federal government to create new recreation infrastructure

Designing and building modern single-track trails

Educating local volunteers and land managers about sustainable trail design

Cultivating local support for recreation projects through outreach and events

Why Become an EOTA Supporter


  • 1. GET AND KEEP ACCESS TO MORE TRAILS- EOTA advocates tirelessly for new trails and protects access to existing trails.

  • 2. SUPPORT TRAIL BUILDING AND MAINTENANCE- Your dollars will help us build and maintain trails all across Eastern Oregon.

  • 3. TOGETHER WE HAVE MORE POWER- More supporters strengthen our voice when we talk to land managers and politicians.

  • 4. KNOW WHAT'S HAPPENING FIRST- Supporters are kept up to date on advocacy issues, events, classes, races and rides.

  • 5. BE PART OF A COMMUNITY- Connect with other trail users and advocates. Meet and ride with like-minded recreationists who support fun, sustainable, legal trails.


As a non-profit organization we rely on donations and charitable contributions from people like yourself to expand quality recreation opportunities across our state. Please consider contributing to our cause today!
